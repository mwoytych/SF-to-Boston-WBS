Work Breakdown Structure

San Francisco → Boston Road Trip – Plan Tasks
1. Trip Planning
1.1 Confirm Travel Dates Decide start and end dates for the cross‑country trip.
1.2 Set Budget Establish total budget for fuel, lodging, food, and contingencies.
1.3 Check Weather Across States Review forecast and seasonal conditions along the planned route.
2. Route & Stops
2.1 Choose Main Route (I‑80 / I‑90) Select primary highways and overall path from San Francisco to Boston.
2.2 Identify Overnight Stops Plan cities or towns for overnight stays along the route.
2.3 Plan Fuel & Food Stops Map out regular fuel, rest, and meal stops.
3. Vehicle Preparation
3.1 Oil Change & Fluids Complete oil change and check coolant, brake, and windshield fluids.
3.2 Tire Check / Spare Inspect tire tread, pressure, and ensure spare and jack are ready.
3.3 Emergency Kit Assemble emergency kit (first aid, tools, jumper cables, flashlight).
4. Packing & Supplies
4.1 Clothing & Essentials Pack clothing, toiletries, and personal essentials for the trip.
4.2 Snacks & Water Prepare non‑perishable snacks and sufficient drinking water.
4.3 Chargers / Navigation Gear Gather phone chargers, GPS/navigation devices, and mounts.
5. Travel Execution
5.1 Depart San Francisco Begin the trip and clear initial departure checklist.
5.2 Daily Driving Blocks Define daily driving durations and rest intervals.
5.3 Track Mileage & Fuel Log miles driven, fuel usage, and costs each day.
6. Arrival & Wrap‑Up
6.1 Arrival in Boston Complete final leg and confirm arrival at destination.
6.2 Unpack & Rest Unload vehicle and rest after the cross‑country drive.
6.3 Trip Review & Notes Reflect on the trip, capture lessons learned, and organize receipts.
